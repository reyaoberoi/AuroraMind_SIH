# AuroraMind

### Digital Mental Health & Psychological Support System for Students

## Overview

AuroraMind is a **digital mental health support platform** designed to address the growing psychological challenges faced by students in higher education. The platform provides a **safe, stigma-free, and always-available digital companion** that supports early identification of mental health risks while enabling institutions to offer timely professional intervention.

Developed as an MVP for **Smart India Hackathon (SIH) 2025**, AuroraMind focuses on accessibility, cultural relevance, and institutional integration within the Indian education ecosystem.

---

## Problem Statement

Mental health issues among students often go undetected due to:

* Social stigma around seeking help
* Limited access to counselors
* Lack of continuous monitoring and early intervention
* Absence of institution-level digital mental health systems

AuroraMind aims to bridge this gap using a **technology-driven, privacy-first solution**.

---

## Proposed Solution

AuroraMind acts as a **digital mental wellness companion** that:

* Provides **24/7 psychological support** without stigma
* Enables **early detection of mental distress** through user interactions
* Connects students with **institution-linked counselors** when needed
* Offers a **safe and confidential environment** for self-expression

Unlike generic mental health apps, AuroraMind is:

* **Culturally contextualized** for Indian students
* **Institution-integrated**, enabling coordinated care
* **Continuously available**, reducing dependency on physical counseling hours

---

## MVP Features
